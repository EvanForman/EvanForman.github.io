<h2>Once Upon a Future logo</h2>

Logo for the serialised fiction series Once Upon a Future, a collaborative fiction project overseen by Michael Robertson. The series takes classical mythology and fairytale stories and recontextualises them for a sci-fi setting, so the logo had to reflect these two sets of totally opposite visual signifiers. When researching the visual language of fairytales in various forms, one staple was illuminated typography reminiscent of medieval manuscripts. It had a strong association with fairytale themes, but it was a malleable enough idea that it could be done in a modern, minimalistic sci-fi style.
